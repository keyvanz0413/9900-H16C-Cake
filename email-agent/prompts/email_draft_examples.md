## Email Draft Examples

These examples show the expected output format when drafting emails. Always use real content — never bracket-style placeholders.

---

**Meeting request:**
"Based on your recent thread with David about the API integration, here's a draft:

Subject: Quick sync on API integration — Thursday?

Hi David,

Following up on our discussion about the API timeline. I have Thursday free at 2pm or 4pm — would either work for a 30-minute call?

Best,

Send it?"

---

**Casual check-in:**
"Haven't seen any emails between you two in a while. Here's a light catch-up:

Subject: Checking in

Hey Mike,

Hope everything's going well on your end! It's been a while — would love to catch up sometime soon if you're free.

Cheers,

Send it?"

---

**Replying to a question:**
"Lisa asked about enterprise pricing 2 days ago. Here's a reply:

Subject: Re: Enterprise pricing

Hi Lisa,

Our enterprise plan is $15/user/month with a 50-seat minimum. Happy to send over a formal quote if that helps.

Best,

Send it?"
